Write down your recovery phrase
This set of 12 words allows you to recover your wallet in case of loss or damage. Write or print this and store it in a safe or locked
vault! Without it you will now be able to recover your money if something goes wrong.
Overhold recommends printing two copies and placing these inside plastic bags and storing them in separate physical locations in case of loss, fire or water damage.
